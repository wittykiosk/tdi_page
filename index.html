About Us

Machine Learning and Deep Learning are becoming necessary tools to gain an edge in almost any business. However, successfully rolling out projects that are based on artificial intelligence is no simple task.

AAI Labs is a company that focuses solely on helping businesses to utilise machine learning and deep learning techniques to optimise their business processes.

How can it help you?

Machine Learning has opened lots of new opportunities to run businesses better. To help you get a better feeling of it, we present a few examples below.

Knowledge Base

Many companies have loads of documents and sites that are difficult to navigate and search. Modern AI systems can take this unstructured data and turn into a queryable knowledge DataLake where simple solutions like Question Answering become possible.

Marketing

Trialing hundreds of different ads or newsletters to find the best performer can be a time-consuming process. Probabilistic decision-making systems would help you make campaign optimisation more efficient.

Resource optimisation

It is a challenge to forecast inventory or other resource utilisation. However, knowing your needs in advance can help you meet your future expectations easier.

Machine Learning systems can forecast your inventory/electric grid/network load utilisation well in advance, giving you enough time to prepare while avoiding unnecessary waste.

Sales

AI/ML systems can help you uncover patterns in your sales data and they could recommend upselling opportunities thus boosting your profits.

Even more, avoiding customer churn is often a key to ensuring the long-term success of the business. Artificial intelligence systems can help you identify such customers and prevent you from losing millions in revenues.

Analytics

You can't improve it if you can't measure it. Sometimes it's really hard to measure your customer satisfaction. However, analysing communication flow (written or spoken) and sentiment can give you the insight that you were missing.
